Become a Member
LACBPE is one of the oldest professional organizations dedicated to supporting underrepresented individuals in engineering.

Established in 1970, our organization has worked to give back to our communities in the Los Angeles area by providing scholarships and STEM readiness programs such as EXCELL. Our goal is to bring Black engineers together to strengthen our communities and increase representation in STEM fields.
Member Benefits
  • Mentorship opportunities
  • Network with Black Professional Engineers in the Los Angeles area
  • Join a community of likeminded individuals
  • Professional Development Workshops
  • Sholarship programs
  • Access to career portal
